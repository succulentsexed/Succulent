Our Curriculum

Our students begin coding with Scratch, a block based programming language designed by MIT. Scratch allows our students to develop the fundamental computer science logic needed to break down complex problems.

After completing Scratch, students progress to WoofJS, a tool we developed for students to transition Scratch to Javascript. Then they continue to our web curriculum and code in HTML, CSS and Javascript.
